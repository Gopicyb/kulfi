FAQ

+

How much will it cost?

To get a better idea of the cost of what you want to build, send us a message and we will come up with a quote depending on what you want built. We’ll ask you a few questions about the nature of the software, what sort of interactivity the software will have, your graphic design needs, etc. Then we’ll be able to give you a ballpark figure. Our team is also flexible in that we can come to your place of business and come up with a firm quote.

+

How long does it take to finish a project?

The time limit of any project is normally dictated by the client. If you have any time limit in mind we will attempt to assemble it for you.

+

How do I outsource my project to Tefro?

The process begins when you contact us with your requirements. We analyze your requirements and respond to you. On the basis of the further discussion, you can choose an engagement model that suits you the best. After that, we begin the process of development.

+

Can you help my site or app look more professional?

Yes. We help revamp old websites and apps to fix deprecated dependencies and give them a modern look.

+

When do I pay?

For most projects, equal payments are made at the start, midway, and the end of the project, but we can work with you to set up a schedule that meets your needs. We understand that this is a big investment and want to help you budget for the expense in whatever way possible. We accept checks, PayPal,Mpesa and all major credit cards.
